How Often Should You Pump?

The cost of pumping is a fraction of the cost of a system replacement. Use this general guide to understand your property's needs.

1-2 Residents

Standard 1,000 Gallon Tank

Every 3-5 Years

Low usage allows for a longer cycle, provided high-quality toilet paper is used and no heavy grease enters the system.

3-4 Residents

Standard 1,000-1,250 Gallon Tank

Every 2-3 Years

This is most common for suburban families. Regular checks on the baffle and filter are highly recommended annually.

5+ Residents

Large 1,500+ Gallon Tank

Every 1-2 Years

High volume usage requires frequent pumping to stay ahead of solid accumulation and prevent drain field saturation.
